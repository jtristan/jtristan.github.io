Scholar

I am a professor and a computer scientist. I do research in formal verification, machine learning, lamguage design and implementation, and inference algorithms. I did my Ph.D. at INRIA, a postdoc at Harvard, and I spent over 8 wondeful years as a research scientist at Oracle Labs. I was twice a visiting faculty at Harvard.

If you are an undergraduate student looking to do some research, please get in touch. Likewise, if you are looking for a postdoc, I am interested!

Research Goal

My main research goal is to study the formal verification (using a proof assistant) of compilers for probabilistic programming languages.
