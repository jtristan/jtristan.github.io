Scholar

I am a principal member of technical staff at Oracle Labs in Boston where I lead a research team in the machine learning group. Previously, I was a post-doctoral fellow at Harvard University and a Ph.D. student at INRIA. I am a senior member of the ACM. I am interested in systems aspects of machine learning such as: probabilistic programming for models like Bayesian networks and conditional random fields, scaling statistical inference algorithms for GPUs or distributed computing, streaming learning algorithms, automated knowledge base construction.
